The Partnership

Two founders. Two continents. One promise.

Care South Solutions and the Jihan Shariff Foundation have joined forces to build a therapy and disability care facility for children in Kajiado, Kenya. This page is about how that partnership works, and why it works.

Care South Solutions

The US side.

Care South Solutions is the work of Tanna Jackson, a global consultant who refuses to see borders as the edge of her responsibility. The company brings resources, partnerships, and a US-based platform to fund and amplify the work happening on the ground in Kenya.

Meet Tanna →

Jihan Shariff Foundation

The Kenya side.

The Jihan Shariff Foundation operates in the Maasai communities of Kajiado, Kenya. Founded by Jihan Shariff, a documentarian, advocate, and community builder, the Foundation has a track record of on-the-ground impact with youth, women, and children with disabilities.

Follow the mission →

The Origin

Action first. Words second.

Tanna and Jihan connected through a shared commitment to practical humanitarian work, not symbolic charity. What began as a conversation about need quickly turned into a decision to act, especially around therapy access and mobility support for children in Kajiado. That early alignment became the foundation for a wider partnership built on trust, speed, and real responsibility.

She said she was still trying to raise money, and I said, I'll do it. I'll buy the wheelchairs.

— Tanna Jackson

That moment defined the partnership. Not a promise. Not a pledge drive. A decision, followed by action. Everything the partnership has done since, and everything it will do, follows the same rule, if it's right, we move.

How the Work Divides

Each side does what it does best.

Care South Solutions handles…

  • Funding and resource mobilization in the US
  • Global consulting and partnership building
  • Content platform and audience growth
  • Reinvestment of content revenue into the Foundation

The Jihan Shariff Foundation handles…

  • On-the-ground operations in Kajiado
  • Relationships with Maasai community leaders
  • Therapy, equipment, and facility management
  • Direct storytelling from the field

The Shared Build

One facility. Both names.

The Kajiado facility will be built under both names, Care South Solutions and the Jihan Shariff Foundation, as a visible, permanent symbol of what happens when two founders decide that borders are not boundaries.
