What We're Building

A place where every child can heal.

Many Maasai children with disabilities in Kajiado can't access therapy, not because the equipment doesn't exist, but because there is nowhere local for them to go. We are changing that. With land identified, partners committed, and the first wheelchairs already in the hands of children who need them, this facility will be the first of its kind in the community.

  • Free therapy and mobility support
  • Local to the Maasai community
  • Built in partnership with the Jihan Shariff Foundation
  • Sustained through ongoing content and community giving
